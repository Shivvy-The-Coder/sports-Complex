Sports in BIT Mesra

BIT's campus life includes a prolific range of sports activities that keep students sharp and focussed, instilling in them a sense of discipline, healthy competition, teamwork and true sportsmanship. “Never give up” is a credo that every BIT-ian lives by. Right from casual face-off among friends, to intra-college and inter-college tournaments organized on a grand scale, students can find multiple opportunities to showcase their prowess in a sport of their choice. The Department of Physical Education and sports regularly organises training camps and courses and is well equipped with appropriate facilities. The campus encourages sports activities ranging from international ones to sports that have originated from the very heart of our country.
